About

Do you love Film and TV and would love to see your stories on the big screen? Are you looking for a course that teaches you the rudiments you need to upskill your screenwriting skill and show your work to producers around the world? This is the course for you. Intensive Course For Originality In Film and Tv is taught by instructors who are screenwriters ... and producers with experience in bringing story concepts to page and screen. This film and TV course takes a ‘learn by doing’ approach where you’ll learn how to write scripts, research concepts, and make your own films and TV programs. At the end of this course, you will have a creative knowledge and diverse portfolio that will boost your career in the entertainment industry and beyond.

What you will learn

  • The main principles of screenwriting.

  • How to create memorable characters.

  • Narrative structure and storytelling.

  • How to write scripts that are worth buying.

Course Benefits

  • Learn the playbook for writing and creating hit films and Tv.

  • Discover the process of finding and creating original script ideas.

  • Get access to our exclusive Producers’ marketplace, where your script will be seen by international producers.

Course Content

  • what is and is not Original

    Learning what is and is not Original will help you lay the groundwork to write a compelling script. This session will help you understand what Originality is in screenwriting

  • Coming up with original ideas/concept

    You will learn how to be original in your ideas and concepts. How originality isn't solely tied to ideas, most times, it is tied to your unique voice and perspective

  • What If? And then?

    You will learn the “What If? And then?” part of creating original ideas and concepts.

  • Subvert the obvious and expand.

    Learn how to subvert an idea and expand this idea into an original concept for your story.

  • Testing idea/concept for originality.

    Being able to stick to an idea/concept till the end of your story is difficult. You will learn how to test for Originality in your script.

  • Developing idea/concept. The unexpected is necessary.

    For every moment in your story, there has to be an unexpected. Learn why your audience should not be able to predict your next storyline.

  • How to use the unexpected to elevate your idea/concept.

    You will learn how to use the unexpected to elevate your idea/concept.

Who is this for:

  • Screenwriters who want to learn how to write and create hit television.

  • Screenwriters who want to learn how to write scripts from original ideas/concepts.

  • Screenwriters who want to increase their chances of creating a marketable script.

Requirements

  • Have a script in progress or completed.

Course FAQ

Do you have an issue that needs resolving? We’d love to help! Please see below for frequently asked questions and answers that will helpfully resolve them.

Each session is 1 hour long. However, you can watch the course afterward at your pace, which may be more than an hour long.

All sessions are interactive. The instructor will lecture for 20-30 mins, and in the remaining minutes, the instructor will engage with participants and answer any questions they may have. At the end of each session, you will be assigned assignments to be turned in at the beginning of the next session.

All sessions will be recorded. Kindly login to your dashboard, and you will see a link where you can rewatch the recording of your missed session.

You should expect to work on your assignment for 30 hours or more each week. The assignments can be anything from worksheets to incorporating that week's assignment into your script. Be ready to present your assignments in the following week's session.
